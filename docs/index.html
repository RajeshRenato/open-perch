Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't

Designed for speed and simplicity. No bloat, no subscriptions, no cloud sync. Just a fast clipboard manager that stays out of your way.

📌

Saved Snippets

Pin your most-used text with custom labels. API keys, email templates, code snippets — always one click away.

📋

Clipboard History

Auto-captures everything you copy. Text and images, up to 100 items. Never lose that thing you copied.

⌨️

Customizable Hotkey

Set your own global shortcut. Navigate with arrows, quick-select with ⌘1-9. Your hands never leave the keyboard.

🔍

Instant Search

Start typing to filter. Search across snippets and history instantly. Find anything in milliseconds.

💾

Backup & Restore

Export your snippets and settings. Restore on any device. Never lose your data when switching machines.

🌍

Cross Platform

Native apps for macOS, Windows, and Linux. Same experience, wherever you work.
